## Shannon estimator papers (downloaded)

- `paninski2003_entropy_mutual_information.pdf` — Paninski (2003), entropy bias bounds
- `jiao2015_minimax_information_functionals.pdf` — Jiao et al. (2015), minimax functionals
- `wu_yang2016_minimax_entropy_large_alphabets.pdf` — Wu & Yang (2016), minimax entropy
- `valiant_valiant2017_estimating_unseen.pdf` — Valiant & Valiant (2017), unseen species
- `nemenman2002_entropy_inference_revisited.pdf` — Nemenman et al. (2002), entropy inference

## Validation reference datasets (documented here)

- `spider_dataset.md` — iNEXT spider abundance data (Sackett et al. 2011, Chao et al.
  2014). Exact abundance vectors for two hemlock forest treatments; pinned Chao1,
  sample coverage, ACE, Shannon, and Hill number values.

- `scipy_conventions.md` — Convention comparison between this package and
  `scipy.spatial.distance`. Documents identical Bray-Curtis, averaging difference
  for Canberra (scipy is unaveraged), and base-2 vs natural-log difference for
  Jensen-Shannon distance. Includes reference numerical values.
